👤 Profile

Dr. Rashed Al Jayousi is a JSPS Postdoctoral Researcher at Tohoku University (2025–2027) and holds a PhD in Management with a strong foundation in economics. His work is grounded in NGO project experience in Palestine and extensive field research on civil society in conflict zones. Prior to this, he served as Assistant Professor at the Arab American University, teaching business administration and quantitative methods. He has also collaborated with NGOs on security‑sector governance initiatives, with a particular focus on processes of institutionalization and accountability in complex environments.
